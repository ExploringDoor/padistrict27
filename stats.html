Where these stats come from

Captains submit box scores after every game via the Score Entry tab — live scoring, GameChanger PDF import, or manual box-score form. Stats roll up overnight. Discrepancies between captain submissions are flagged to admin (see Score Discrepancy Inbox) and held out of leaderboards until resolved. League presidents can audit their own league's submissions.

Privacy: only first name + last initial shown for players under 13 unless parents have opted in via Player Settings.
