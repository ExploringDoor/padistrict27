Required Protocols

🧠 Concussion Protocol PA Mandate

Per the PA Safety in Youth Sports Act: Any player suspected of a concussion must be removed from play immediately and may not return until cleared by a medical professional. All D27 coaches certified annually.

  1. Remove player from play immediately. No return-to-play that day.
  2. Notify parent / guardian. Document on D27 Incident Log within 24 hours.
  3. Player needs written medical clearance before returning to any practice or game.
  4. Submit return-to-play form to league president and D27 Safety Officer.

☀ Heat Illness Protocol LL Required

Heat-index thresholds (WBGT, not just air temp) determine play status. D27 fields auto-monitor.

  1. Below 82°F WBGT: normal play.
  2. 82–87°F: 5-minute hydration break each inning.
  3. 88–90°F: mandatory 10-minute break every 30 min · pitchers limited to 50% normal pitch count.
  4. Above 90°F: game suspended · automatic notification to all D27 captains.

⚡ Lightning & Severe Weather LL Required

D27 uses real-time NOAA + per-field detectors. Trigger: lightning within 10 miles. Status auto-broadcast.

  1. Lightning < 10 miles: notify umpires, captains, and parents · move toward shelter.
  2. Lightning < 8 miles: game suspended. All players, coaches, fans to indoor shelter or vehicles.
  3. Resume only after 30 consecutive minutes of no lightning within 8 miles.
  4. Cancellation/postponement notice auto-sent district-wide via push + SMS + email.

🩹 Bleeding / Open Wound Protocol

Per LL rule, any player with bleeding wounds must leave play, be treated, and have all blood-stained items replaced before returning.

  1. Player exits field immediately.
  2. Treat with First Aid. Use gloves (in dugout kit).
  3. Replace any blood-stained jerseys / equipment.
  4. Return only after bleeding fully stopped + dressing applied.

👮 Suspected Abuse Reporting PA Mandated Reporter

All D27 coaches and managers are mandated reporters under PA Child Protective Services Law. Suspected abuse must be reported within 24 hours.

  1. Call PA ChildLine: 1-800-932-0313 (24 hours).
  2. Notify D27 Safety Officer privately within 24 hours.
  3. Document factually only. No investigation by D27.
  4. Confidentiality preserved. Anonymous reporting accepted.
