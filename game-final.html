Recap

DiSanto, Wu lead top-seeded Berwyn-Paoli past Pottstown 12-2 in four

Berwyn-Paoli wasted no time Saturday evening at Monument Avenue, plating three runs in the top of the first on a Luke DiSanto three-run homer and never looking back in a 12-2 mercy-rule win over Pottstown that sends the #1 seed to Wednesday's semifinal against Devon-Strafford.

DiSanto finished 3-for-3 with a homer and five RBI. Catcher Ethan Pierce added a two-run shot in the third and drove in three. On the mound, Caleb Wu went the distance — a four-inning, six-strikeout complete game on 58 pitches, well inside his Majors eligibility window — giving up a solo shot to leadoff man M. Rossi in the second and an unearned run in the third.

Berwyn-Paoli (now 10-0) next face #4 Devon-Strafford in Semifinal 1, Thursday June 25 at 6:30 PM. Pottstown ends the tournament at 6-4.
