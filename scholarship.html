About the Scholarship

Established in 2005, the Laura and Van Scott PA District 27 Little League Higher Education Scholarship provides financial aid to District 27 baseball and softball participants continuing their education. Two awards are given each year — one for baseball and one for softball — most recently $1,000 each.

Who's Eligible

  • Participated for four or more years in a PA District 27 Little League
  • A high school junior, senior, or first-year college student
  • Pursuing post-secondary education at an accredited college, trade, vocational, or technical school
  • Submits a completed application by the deadline

Eligible leagues: Berwyn-Paoli, Chester Valley, Coventry, Devon-Strafford, Exton, Great Valley, Lower Merion, Lower Perkiomen, Pottsgrove, Pottstown, Radnor-Wayne, and Upper Providence.

Selection

Applications are de-identified before review — the committee votes without knowing applicants' names or league affiliations. Winners are announced at the District 27 Annual Banquet.
