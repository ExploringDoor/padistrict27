About Laura & Van Scott

Who this scholarship is for
Est. 1993 · D27
L & V
Laura & Van
Scott

Van · 1st DA of D27 Laura · Financial Coordinator

Van and Laura Scott were founding members of Pottstown Little League. Van served as Assistant District Administrator under Tony Marra in the old District 23. When D23 grew too large and split in 1993, Van became the first District Administrator of PA District 27 — and Laura took on the role of D27's first Financial Coordinator. They served together for the next eleven years.

They stepped down from district roles in 2004. A year later, this scholarship was endowed in their name — a gift from the leagues they had quietly held together. It's awarded every October at the annual banquet, announced by the scholarship chair to the two graduating players who best reflect what Van and Laura stood for: showing up, season after season, for the kids and the leagues.

Eligibility

  • Minimum of 4 years playing in a PA District 27 Little League
  • Current high school junior or senior, or a first-year college student
  • Pursuing post-secondary education — college, trade school, or certification program
  • A letter of recommendation from a D27 league officer, coach, or umpire
  • One page: why Little League shaped who you are, and what's next

How to Apply

  1. Download the application packet from bpall.org / d27 hub (opens April 1)
  2. Complete the application, essay, and recommendation letter
  3. Email or mail to Suzanne Battaglia, Scholarship Chair
  4. Postmark or send by September 8
  5. Recipients notified by phone in late September; announced at the banquet
