Nominate for the Class of 2027

Eligible: any D27 player (5 yr post-LL eligibility), coach (10+ years), volunteer (15+ years), or pioneer. Nominations open Jan 1, due Aug 31. Inductee class announced at the October banquet.

Nominate →

Selection Criteria

  • Players — Excellence on field (D27 + post-LL career) · 5 years post-eligibility (no current youth players) · sportsmanship-by-default standard
  • Coaches/Managers — Minimum 10 years actively coaching at a D27 league · documented player development impact · clean disciplinary record
  • Volunteers — 15+ years of service · field crew, board members, scorekeepers, snack bar coordinators, all welcome · proven district-wide impact
  • Pioneers — Founding-era contribution · league/charter foundation · institutional knowledge transferred · usually inducted posthumously

Selection committee: Jeff Bennett (DA · chair), Jason Saylor (ADA), 12 league presidents, 3 elected at-large members. Voting confidential. New class capped at 4 inductees per year to preserve standard.
