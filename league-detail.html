Welcome to Berwyn-Paoli

For over 60 years, Berwyn-Paoli Little League has been the backbone of youth baseball and softball on Chester County's Main Line. Every spring we welcome 600+ players ages 4–16 onto the green diamond at Field of Dreams, and every summer we send our best tournament teams to compete across PA District 27.

Whether your child is picking up a glove for the very first time or chasing a sectional title, our coaches, volunteers, and umpires are here to make this the best season of their year.

— Tom Coleman, League President
