About the Tournament

The Bob Jones Tournament has been the signature regular-season event of District 27 for over two decades. Named for the longtime Bridgeport Little League President, it pulls the top Majors-division team from each member league into a single-elimination bracket — the only district-wide baseball showcase that happens before the all-star season begins.
