Welcome to Radnor-Wayne

For nearly six decades, Radnor-Wayne Little League has served the Worcester/Audubon/Eagleville corridor of western Montgomery County. We field 42 teams across baseball and softball — Tee Ball through Senior League — at our home complex on Indian Creek Road.

Radnor-Wayne has won three D27 championships since 2018 and consistently sends competitive All-Star teams to Section 8. We pride ourselves on player-development pitching mechanics, mandatory rotation through positions in younger divisions, and a no-snap-decision umpire-respect policy that makes our complex a destination for traveling tournaments.
