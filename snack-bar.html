The deal

One family = one shift per season. Trade with another family if your week doesn't work. Cancellations < 48 hours get reassigned to a backup. League cellphone (handled by snack-bar coordinator) goes home with the closing shift to lock up.

Drinks, hot dogs, pretzels, candy, Gatorade. League supplies — you don't shop, you sell. Tips go in a jar. End-of-night count + drop into the lockbox. Treasury page shows running totals.
