Violations

Real-time aggregation across all 12 leagues. If a captain tries to log a pitcher who isn't eligible, the score-entry screen blocks submission and routes to the admin for resolution. Replaces the manual paper Pitch Affidavit at tournament check-in.

Why this matters

Little League International requires districts to enforce these limits. Manual tracking in spreadsheets misses cross-team appearances during All-Stars. Arm injury prevention for kids is the #1 reason these rules exist.

Auto-Affidavit

System generates the LL Pitch Affidavit form automatically before each tournament game. Plate ump signs digitally. No more lost paperwork at check-in.

Captain View

Coach Tom sees a green/yellow/red light per pitcher when building tomorrow's lineup. No more "wait, did Jack throw 41 or 51?" texts at 11 PM.

Parent Notifications

Optional: parents get a weekly digest of their kid's pitch counts and arm-rest status.
