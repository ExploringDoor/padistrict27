How D27 Tryouts Work

All D27 leagues use evaluation-based player placement to keep teams balanced. Players attend tryouts, are evaluated by coaches and coordinators on hitting, fielding, throwing, and (for older divisions) pitching. League player agents then handle a draft to balance teams. Younger divisions (T-ball, Coach Pitch, 7-8U) typically don't tryout — friend requests honored when possible. Late registration accepted with smaller chance of preferred placement.

2027 Registration

Opens Nov 15, 2026 at 8 AM. Early-bird ends Dec 31. Each D27 league handles its own boundary check + roster formation.

Fees

Range $135–$295 per player depending on age + league. Multi-child discounts. Scholarship available for any family — see Scott Scholarship. No family pays for need.

Boundaries

Little League is residency-based. You play for your chartered league. Use the LL boundary tool or D27 league finder.
