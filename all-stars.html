D27 All-Star Tournament Teams

Selected from each chartered league, our All-Star teams represent District 27 in Little League's regional tournament path — Section 8 → State → Mid-Atlantic Region → Williamsport. Rosters confirmed June 1.

Eligibility & Residency

Players must reside within their league's chartered boundary as of June 1 and be aged 8–16 (per LL age cutoff Aug 31). Each league may field one team per division. Tournament rules: re-entry not allowed, mandatory play, full pitch-count enforcement (see Pitch Count). All players must have valid residency documentation on file.
