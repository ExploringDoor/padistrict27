Volunteer Hours · 2025 Season

Why volunteer?

It's the only reason this whole thing works. Twelve leagues, 10,000 kids — the parents and neighbors who show up are the real engine. Most volunteers find a role and stay 5+ years.

What we'll do

You sign up, we match you with your league's coordinator within 48 hours. They'll walk you through background check + Safesport. Most volunteers are on a field within two weeks.

Coaching specifically

Want to coach? Hit "Coaching" on the form, we'll route you to your league president. They run tryout-style assessments mid-March each year.
