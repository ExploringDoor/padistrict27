How it works (production)

Each league's field crew has a magic-link bookmark. One tap toggles status. Notifications fan out to scheduled games' captains, umpires, and parents on that day. District-wide weather radar layered on top. Historical log per field (last 30 days) for grant applications and grounds-crew planning.
