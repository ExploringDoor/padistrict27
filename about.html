About District 27

Leadership and guidance to locally chartered Little Leagues across Chester, Delaware, and Montgomery counties.

PA District 27 is one of Pennsylvania's Little League districts, supporting the locally chartered leagues that run baseball and softball across Chester, Delaware, and Montgomery counties. The District organizes and administers the postseason tournaments — International divisions, the District's Special Games, and the road through Section 8 toward the state tournament.

District 27's twelve member leagues field thousands of players each summer. Several of the District's signature tournaments carry the names of the volunteers who built the game here — stories you can read on the History page.
