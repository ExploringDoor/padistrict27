Required Certifications · Quick Reference

Background CheckJDP / NCSI nationwide. Required annually for any adult in dugout or with player access.
Safesport / Abuse AwarenessFree online module. ~30 min. Required annually per LL Reg I(c).
Concussion AwarenessPA Safety in Youth Sports Act. Required for all coaches before season.
CDC Heads UpCompanion concussion training. CDC-issued certificate, 3-year cycle.
First Aid / CPRRecommended; required for tournament dugouts. 2-year cycle.
Coaching EducationOptional unless tournament — Diamond Leader / ABCA module.

Quick Actions

Why this matters

Little League International audits chartered districts annually. A single uncleared coach in the dugout during a sanctioned game can void insurance, force forfeit, and trigger LL revocation. Spreadsheet tracking misses lapses.

League Submission

Each league president sees only their own coaches. They submit cert renewals here; D27 admin sees aggregate compliance and audit-ready reports.
