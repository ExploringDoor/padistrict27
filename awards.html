Coach of the Year

Honored for impact on player development, sportsmanship, and contribution beyond wins and losses.

Tom Caldwell · BP Red 12U
2026 Coach of the Year · 9-1 regular season · Bob Jones Tournament finalist
Tom rebuilt the Berwyn-Paoli 12U program from the bottom up over three years — installing year-round skills clinics, partnering with Vetri's culinary nutrition program, and championing the league's first concussion-protocol pilot. His teams have earned three straight Sportsmanship awards. Tom mentors first-year managers across D27 and personally certified 18 new coaches in 2026.
Sam Riccardo · Radnor-Wayne 12U
2026 Coach of the Year (B Division) · 9-1 regular season
Sam moved Radnor-Wayne into the top of the standings while running the largest 10U–12U pitch-mechanics clinic in the district. Pulled five young arms back from overuse trends with custom workload plans. His Radnor-Wayne Maroon roster averaged 33 fewer pitches per game than league average — without sacrificing wins.
